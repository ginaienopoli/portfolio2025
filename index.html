About

Photographer, Illustrator, & Designer

Gina Ienopoli, a New York-based artist renowned for her creative talents in fine arts and e-commerce, discovered her passion for art at a young age. Raised on Long Island by an artistic family, she began drawing as a child and experimented with photography at age twelve. In high school, Ienopoli expanded her knowledge of photo manipulation and fundamentals through an introduction to Adobe Photoshop and film development, winning photography awards along the way. Her skills reached new heights when she started using a Wacom tablet to digitally paint in Adobe software, sparking a passion for digital art. Through art journaling and self-expression, Ienopoli built an impressive portfolio and was admitted to the Fashion Institute of Technology. There, she earned an Associate of Applied Science in Illustration and a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Animation and Interactive Media, graduating with honors. After graduating, she returned to photography, honing her studio portrait and e-commerce skills over four years of photoshoots. Now embarking on freelancing while educating herself in digital marketing, Ienopoli is eager to leverage her diverse artistic talents to help fellow artists and clients.
