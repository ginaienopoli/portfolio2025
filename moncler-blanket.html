Moncler Blanket

Photographed in Hirshleifers' Photo Studio

The inherent convenience of the Moncler blanket, particularly its ability to fold neatly into a self-contained bag, was a key focus for this shoot. Our aim was to highlight its practical utility for outdoor activities such as camping and efficient packing.

Although the studio's limited space posed a challenge in capturing a broader perspective of the unfolded image, we implemented innovative solutions, including attaching clips to the blanket on the c-stands, to achieve the desired outcome.

In the post-production phase in Photoshop, I refined the images using the liquify tool to enhance the product's shape and the pen tool to create an accurate clipping mask. A clean white background was then added behind the product to maintain stylistic consistency across the company’s website. This thorough approach effectively showcased the product's design and functionality while aligning with brand aesthetic standards.
