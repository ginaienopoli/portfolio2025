Enamel Pin of Lucky from Animal Crossing

Photographed in personal photo studio

Etsy Listing

This pin design draws inspiration from a casual video game known as Animal Crossing, featuring a character named "Lucky". With a darker twist, Lucky is transformed from his original design of a mummified dog into a mummified dog with protruding, sharp teeth and an elongated tongue. This accessory is ideal for fans of Animal Crossing and those who have an appreciation for eerie designs.

The photographs were captured in a studio setting, showcasing the pin on draped orange fabric, illuminated by softboxes and strobes.

This series of images includes a front view of the pin, a three-quarters view with reflection, a back view, a sketch of the original design, and a rendering of the final design.
