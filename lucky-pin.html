Enamel Pin of Lucky from Animal Crossing

Designed and Photographed by Gina Ienopoli

Etsy Listing

This pin design was inspired by a videogame called Animal Crossing, with a character named "Lucky" featured in the game. With a dark twist Lucky is changed from his original character design by having sharp teeth and a long tongue. Perfect accessory for Halloween or a goth.

This pin was taken in studio on a draped orange fabric using softboxes, and strobes.
