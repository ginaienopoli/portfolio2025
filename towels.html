Towels Set

Photographed in Safavieh's Photo Studio

The most recent collection from Safavieh features high-quality towels available in a range of colors. My stylist and I had the opportunity to photograph and style these towels. The package includes two bath towels, two hand towels, and four face towels. During the shoot, my stylist arranged the towels and inserted cardboard into the folds to create a flatter and more uniform appearance. After capturing the towel set from different angles and taking detailed shots, I utilized Adobe Photoshop to address any imperfections using the liquify, healing, clipping path, and clone stamp tools.

The subsequent images include a front view of the complete set, a three-quarter view of the set, a three-quarter view with one towel unfolded, a detailed close-up, and a swatch view.
